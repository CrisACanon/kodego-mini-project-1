Now Delivering Nationwide!

NOW, through strategic partnership with our 3rd party Logistics Provider, we offer Nationwide Delivery Coverage which allows us to serve the rest of Luzon as well as Visayas and Mindanao. For these additional areas, our standard delivery lead times (as shown below) will apply.

Same Day Delivery

successful placed orders are verified and delivered to your designated address within less than 24 hours. (Orders placed from 12:00am to 12:00nn) within in the areas of SOCKSARGEN only, (South Cotabato, Sultan Kudarat, Sarangani Province and General Santos City.)

Standard Delivery

successful placed orders are verified and delivered to your designated address within the estimated delivery days except during Holidays. 2-3 days for Mindanao, 3-7 days for Visayas and 7-10 days for Luzon.

Important Notes:

1. In case you will not be able to personally receive your order, please make sure to prepare an authorization letter and proof of your authorized personâ€™s identity (e.g. any government-issued ID). Please check whether all of your ordered items are delivered with what's stated in the delivery note, and then sign on the delivery note to acknowledge receipt before returning it to our logistics partner. Notification will be sent to keep you updated on the order status. You can also log in to your account to check your order status any time.

2. In case of undesirable weather conditions or delivery limitations due to local area restrictions, deliveries may be delayed. Nonetheless, we will keep you informed and we will make most of our effort to deliver your order at the soonest possible time.

COD (Cash on Delivery) is applicable only for orders below P50,000 within SOCKSARGEN only. (South Cotabato, Sultan Kudarat, Sarangani Province and General Santos City), while P30,000 within remaining part of Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao.

Disclaimer: For items that are identified for Special Handling, a Customer Service Associate will reach out to you to discuss the fulfillment and delivery details

- Multiple orders placed with same delivery address.
